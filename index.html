Our Mission
STL Clean & Green is a STL based business providing premier pressure washing services to homes across Missouri area since 2017. This ecofriendly company was founded and has been under the leadership of Nathan Smithee, an STL native. While scrolling through the website, hopefully any questions you have about what STL Clean and Green can do for you will be answered. If you would like to inquire about our services please refer to the Contact Us section!
Our Services
Residential
STL Clean and Green is here to help make you feel like your home is able to redefine curb appeal. This specialized system is able to remove the debris, grime, and various external marks that occur over time. Not only do we want to see results from utilizing our state of the art equipment but more importantly we provide education and care in preventing any secondary effects that can come from high pressure washing on homes, buildings, any place where water can become a problem.
Commercial
Our client base is diverse; however, with each visit it is paramount that a solid relationship based on communication is created with each our clients. We are here to help your house have it’s best shine, and to keep it that way!
Offering Reliable Power Washing Services
Pressure washing machinery used by STL C&G is specialized to use high temperature water that is soft. This is important for many reasons, but the most important is mold prevention. Without having to use excessive force, minimizing the amount of moisture that potentially can seep in between siding, around windows, even including the roof, our goal is to address the main concern and to prevent further issues from developing in the future.
